|Opening tag|Attribute property|Attribute value|Content|Closing tag|
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
|`<a`|`href=`|`"https://ucr.fbi.gov/">`|FBI Uniform Crime Reporting|`</a>`|
|`<h2`|`class=`|`"subhead">`|Authorities investigating cause of incident|`</h2>`|

`<` `=` `""` `>` `<` `/` `>`
